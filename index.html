COOLING UNIT TYPE

A wall unit is mounted on the wall of the cell (inside and outside). A ceiling unit is mounted in the ceiling of the cell (inside and outside). A split unit consists of a part on the inside of the cell and a part outside the building, connected to each other by means of piping.

STRIP CURTAIN

A strip curtain ensures less cold loss when the door is opened and therefore saves energy.

FLOOR BEAMS

We particularly recommend floor beams for freezers. These provide extra insulation and less condensation. Floor beams are placed under the cell and are approx. 40 - 45 mm high.
